How our project will bring
value to the space

The Super Soldiers 3D collection is a PFP NFT with new, unique and high quality artwork but that's not all. We are here to be a project to deliver innovation to the Web3 space. One of the main concerns that cryptocurrency and blockchain aims to tackle is centralisation of power and control. The future is now and we are here to improve decentralization across the globe.

Our ambition is to develop an open source blockchain voting system utilizing tokens. This is in aspiration to make the world a fairer place by moving power away from the wealthy and powerful and spreading it across the larger population. This technology will make it possible to take any sort of poll, voting, data collection, elections and more without any sort of manipulation or corruption.

By owning one of our Super Soldier 3D NFTs it means you are investing and taking part in a community that is here to bring about change, innovation and the development of this technology. The members of our community will be the very first to be able to test our voting system as we develop it into its final stages and officially release it to the public. Members will be able to have a say and vote in how our project develops in the long term.

Applications

DAO

As the space develops, most projects, especially NFT projects aim to become a DAO (Decentralized Autonomous Organization) where the control, power and direction the project develops is in the hands of the members of the community rather than giving complete control to founders/leaders. This application is perfect for this voting system as it will allow the project team to collect accurate, unmanipulated data that is also transparent to the people ensuring the project truly is decentralized.

Case Study

Terra Luna & Luna 2.0 Hard Fork

In early May 2022 cryptocurrency Terra LUNA, founded by TerraForm Labs directed by Do Kwon collapsed. This was a catastrophic event for the crypto space and contributed to the market crashing significantly. With the coin in turmoil director Do Kwon decided to carry out a poll for LUNA holders/investors to determine the future of the project. Following many allegations against Do Kwon, most believe this poll was rigged. Many individuals in the crypto community surrounding the downfall of LUNA stated there were already more than 10 million 'YES' votes only moments after the poll was opened. Chances are, this most likely was rigged.

With a new open source blockchain voting system this will remove the worry of having the votes manipulated by an individual such as Do Kwon. Everyone will be able to see the tokens and transactions clearly ensuring that the vote will not be rigged. If this system was in place, at the least all the people that fell victim would have an actual say in what happens next with the project they invested in.
