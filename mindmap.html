Super Soldiers 3D Collection Mint

The Super Soldiers 3D collection will be produced and made available for the community to mint on the Solana Blockchain. Minting details will be announced on all our platforms. It will be made very clear when we mint, do not mint through any links besides our official links in discord, socials and linktree. Stay safe.

Super Crates Deployment

The Super Soldiers Team will be releasing mysterious Super Crates containing a random item. Inside you will find brand new weapons and gun skins. Holders will be distributed a free Super Crate each post mint.

Super Soldier Weapon Customisation

The contents of Super Crates will be applicable to be combined with each existing Super Soldier NFT. This means we will be implementing a function on our website to allow holders to swap out their original weapon with a brand new one they unboxed from a Super Crate. This will be deployed post mint.

Treasury

A small portion of Super Soldiers 3D NFT collection will be airdropped to the team wallet. This will act as the project treasury and will be used in future development of the project. NFTs in the treasury will be used to collaborate with other projects to develop and expand our brand. Not to mention we will be giving back to the community too, expect plenty of giveaways. 200 (2%) units will be held by the team with 100 dedicated to the community treasury and 100 for staff.

Royalties

Throughout the secondary market transaction royalties will be set to 5%. This means every sale that occurs 5% of the sale will be distributed to the Super Soldiers treasury. Treasury funds will continuously be reinvested into further development and maintenance of the project.

Project Development

Safety

Currently there is no released information about the Super Soldiers 3D mint. Announcements will be made very clearly on our social media platforms, discord and website before the minting occurs. We are not responsible if you click a foreign link and have funds stolen. Only use official links on our website, discord and social media. Please be utmost careful and stay safe.

Collection

The project is launching the Super Soldiers 3D NFT collection on the Solana Blockchain. There will be a supply of 10,000 NFTs to be minted. Our high quality soldiers have been professionally developed by our graphic designer to complete a collection with over 220 unique traits and variations. In the collection there will be ten Elite Soldiers to be minted. They are one of ones, and are extremely rare with individually unique traits.

Sales

During the mint there will be a public sale and whitelist sale. Being on the whitelist grants you access to the exclusive whitelist sale in which you will be granted a discounted mint. Pricing TBA with minting information.

We are a project that doesn't believe in extensive hours of work just to join a whitelist hence we have made it achievable for everyone to join the whitelist. Details on whitelist requirements will be made available on our Discord. Whitelist spots will also be regularly given away on our social media platforms. So be sure to stay active there.
